Welcome to DFSharp!

Below, you'll find an interactive lineup optimizer for NBA DFS.

The projections used are updated with live injury information, and the ownership information is live as well, courtesy of OwnThePlay

Addditional graphs, news, and live analysis can found Here

Methodology behind the creation of this site, and the accompanying dashboard, can be found Here

This site is open source (code is Here), in active development and new features are added often, so stay tuned for more, and happy optimizing!

Keep in mind when building lineups factoring ownership, that ownership information is most useful closer to tip-off, generally from 5-7PM.
